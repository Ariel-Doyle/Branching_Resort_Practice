Travel Arrangements

We want only the smoothest options for travel to and from both the island and the resort, that's why we work exclusively with Travel To Paradise

If you should choose to plan your stay with us on your own, we are accessible by all the major taxi services. The bus route 29 has a stop at the bottom of the hill (stop 326), after which we can send out a shuttle to retrieve you and your luggage. Please call (111)222-3333 EXT 447 to make arrangements with the resort.
